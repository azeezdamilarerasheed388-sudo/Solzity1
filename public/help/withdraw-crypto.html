How to Withdraw Crypto

  1. Go to Withdrawal page

    Click "Withdraw" from the dashboard or menu

  2. Select token

    Choose SOL, USDC, or USDT

  3. Enter withdrawal address

    Paste your external wallet address (Solana network)

  4. Enter amount

    Minimum: 0.01 SOL / 10 USDC / 10 USDT

  5. Confirm withdrawal

    Review details and confirm. Funds are sent after approval.

💰 Withdrawal Fees:

SOL: 0.01 SOL • USDC: $3 • USDT: $3

Processing time: 1-2 hours
