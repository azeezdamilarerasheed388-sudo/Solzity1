Stop Loss & Take Profit Orders

🛑 Stop Loss

Automatically closes your position at a specified loss level to protect your capital.

Example: Buy at $100 with stop loss at $95 → position closes if price drops to $95

✅ Take Profit

Automatically closes your position at a specified profit target.

Example: Buy at $100 with take profit at $110 → position closes if price reaches $110

How to Set SL/TP

  1. Click "Click to enable" in the SL/TP section
  2. Enter your Stop Loss price
  3. Enter your Take Profit price
  4. Click "Buy with SL/TP" or "Sell with SL/TP"
