Account Lockout

Your account may be temporarily locked after 5 failed login attempts

Why does this happen?

This security measure protects your account from brute force attacks.

What to do:

  1. Wait 15 minutes - lockout automatically expires
  2. Use "Forgot Password" if you don't remember your password
  3. Contact support if issues persist

💡 Tip: Use password manager to avoid failed attempts
