⚠️ Liquidation

Liquidation happens when your margin level falls below 10%

What is Margin Level?

Margin Level = (Equity / Used Margin) × 100%

Example

  • You have $100 trading balance
  • You open a position requiring $50 margin
  • If losses reduce equity to $5, margin level = 10%
  • Position is liquidated to prevent negative balance

💡 Tip: Always monitor your margin level and use Stop Loss orders!
