How to Start Trading with 100x Leverage

What is Leverage Trading?

Leverage allows you to trade positions larger than your account balance. With 100x leverage, a $10 margin lets you control a $1,000 position.

Step 1: Fund Your Trading Account

Transfer USDC or USDT from your main wallet to your trading balance. Go to the trading page and use the "↓ To Trading" button.

Step 2: Choose Your Market

Select from available pairs: BTC/USDT, ETH/USDT, ETC/USDT, or SOL/USDT.

Step 3: Set Your Volume

Choose your position size. The required margin will be calculated automatically based on 100x leverage.

Step 4: Place Your Trade

Click BUY if you expect prices to rise, or SELL if you expect prices to fall.

Risk Management

Always use Stop Loss orders to protect your capital. Set take profit targets to secure gains.
