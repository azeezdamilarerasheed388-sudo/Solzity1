How to Withdraw NGN to Your Bank

  1. Go to NGN Wallet

  2. Click "Withdraw"

    Enter amount (minimum ₦1,000)

  3. Select your bank

    Choose from the list of Nigerian banks

  4. Enter account number

    Account name will auto-verify

  5. Confirm withdrawal

    Funds are sent to your bank account (1-2 hours)

📋 Processing Time:

Withdrawals are processed within 1-2 hours during business days
