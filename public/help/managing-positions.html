Managing Your Positions

View Open Positions

All your open positions are displayed below the trading panel. Each card shows:

  • Asset and side (BUY/SELL)
  • Volume and entry price
  • Current price and P&L
  • Margin used

Close a Position

Click the "Close" button on any position card to close it manually.

Monitor Your P&L

Green P&L = Profit • Red P&L = Loss • Numbers update in real-time

View Trading History

Go to History page to see all your closed positions and trading performance.
