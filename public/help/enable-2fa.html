How to Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

  1. Go to Profile → Security

  2. Click "Enable 2FA"

  3. Scan QR code with Google Authenticator

    Download Google Authenticator from App Store or Play Store

  4. Enter the 6-digit code

    Verify by entering the code from your authenticator app

  5. Save backup codes

    Store backup codes safely in case you lose access to your phone

✅ 2FA adds an extra layer of security to your account
