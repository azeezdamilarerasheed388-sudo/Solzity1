How to Deposit Crypto

Step 1: Get Your Deposit Address

On your dashboard, you'll see your deposit address for SOL, USDC, and USDT.

Example: 7x4d...8f3k (always verify the full address)

Step 2: Send from External Wallet

Use any Solana-compatible wallet (Phantom, Solflare, etc.) to send funds to your deposit address.

Step 3: Wait for Confirmation

Deposits are credited after network confirmations (usually 1-2 minutes on Solana).

⚠️ Important:

  • Only send Solana network tokens (SPL tokens)
  • Minimum deposit: 0.01 SOL / 1 USDC / 1 USDT
  • Always verify the address before sending
