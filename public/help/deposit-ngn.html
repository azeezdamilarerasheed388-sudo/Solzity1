How to Deposit NGN via Paystack

💳 Instant deposits via bank transfer, card, or USSD

  1. Go to NGN Wallet

    Click on "NGN Wallet" in the menu

  2. Click "Deposit NGN"

    Enter the amount you want to deposit (minimum ₦100)

  3. Choose payment method

    You'll be redirected to Paystack to complete payment via bank transfer, card, or USSD

  4. Complete payment

    Follow Paystack instructions to complete your deposit

  5. Funds credited instantly

    Once payment is confirmed, your NGN balance updates automatically
