NGN Wallet Overview

The NGN Wallet allows Nigerian users to deposit and withdraw in Naira, and convert to/from USDC and USDT.

💰 Exchange Rates

Buy USDC/USDT: Real rate - 80 spread

Sell USDC/USDT: Real rate

Example: If real rate is ₦1,500, you buy at ₦1,420, sell at ₦1,500

💳 Features

  • Deposit via bank transfer, card, or USSD (Paystack)
  • Withdraw to Nigerian banks
  • Convert between NGN and USDC/USDT
  • Minimum deposit: ₦100
  • Minimum withdrawal: ₦1,000
