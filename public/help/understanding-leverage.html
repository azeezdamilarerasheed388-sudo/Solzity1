Understanding 100x Leverage

⚠️ Leverage amplifies both profits AND losses

How Leverage Works

With 100x leverage, a 1% price movement results in a 100% profit or loss on your margin.

Profit Example

$10 margin → $1,000 position
1% price increase → $10 profit (100% return)

Loss Example

$10 margin → $1,000 position
1% price decrease → $10 loss (100% loss)

Liquidation

If losses reach your entire margin, your position will be automatically closed (liquidated) to prevent negative balance.
