Trading cryptocurrencies involves substantial risk. You could lose all your funds.

1. Leverage Risk

Our platform offers up to 100x leverage. While leverage can amplify profits, it also amplifies losses. A small market movement against your position can result in total loss of your margin.

2. Liquidation Risk

If your margin level falls below 10%, your positions will be automatically liquidated. You may lose your entire margin balance.

3. Market Risk

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile. Prices can change rapidly and dramatically. You should be prepared for the possibility of losing all invested funds.

4. Liquidity Risk

During extreme market conditions, you may not be able to close positions at desired prices. This could result in larger losses than expected.

5. Technology Risk

The platform relies on internet connectivity, blockchain networks, and third-party services. Technical failures could delay or prevent transactions.

6. Regulatory Risk

Cryptocurrency regulations vary by jurisdiction and may change. Future regulations could affect the availability or operation of our services.

We do not provide investment advice. All trading decisions are your own. You should consult with financial advisors before trading.
